Nostalgia

medium: Net Art date: 04 April 2018

I personally did not feel up to the task of this project not because of the coding, but more on the time I will put into one page when I have so many more important things to finish then perfecting the 1st part of a project. It made me somewhat stressed because there was a lot I could work with from all the resources the internet has to offer and usually when I do become stressed I start to wander around or write down ideas in my notebook, I usally have pretty good ideas, but when deciding on what to make for this project was the most difficult because I had a good amount of ideas that I can work with, with the time I had which was at least four days I thought that was going to be enough time, but I was sadly wrong. I wanted to add not just 3-4 webpages, but at least 6-9 pages. My concept for what I wanted to do was make a arcadic Nostalgic website. I wanted to somewhat make an arcade machine website of Nostalgia, but it didn't go as plan. Originally I wanted the token to take random people to different webpages where either it is a little game of space invaders or a page full of old wholesome cartoon and TV shows where another one might also get a collage of different photos of things people grew up with as a kid whether it was a toy, or shows, or their favorite snack with another token at the end of the page going back to where you originally started from. Though I could not find out how to randomize the tokens placements so I had to not keep trying to waste time with it. I was able to code some of the games, but did not really have time to put them within my webpage. Though now that I understand how things now fuction, I'm debating to have this webpage grow slowly on the back burner
